Jazz Standards in the U.S. Public Domain

In the United States, compositions published 95 years ago are in the public domain. This includes many jazz standards. Freed Jazz catalogues and publishes lead sheets for these tunes. All of our work is itself released into the public domain, unencumbered by U.S. copyright restrictions and free of cost.
