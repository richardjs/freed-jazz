What does Freed Jazz do?

We aim to be a comprehensive catalog of jazz standards in the U.S. public domain. For each standard, we create a lead sheet based on its public domain publication and add standard contemporary chord changes. We also add lyrics and provide transpositions for every key. All of Freed Jazz's work is itself released into the public domain.

Our work builds on top of IMSLP and is inspired by Standard Ebooks.
